word (w α : d)

(noun)

 

1. a single distinct meaningful unit of language

2. a basic unit of data in a computer

3. something spoken or written: a word of warning

4. (with negative) the smallest amount of something spoken or written: don’t believe a word of it

5. contentious or angry speech: he had words with her

6. a command, password, or signal: she gave the word to begin

7. one’s account of the truth: her word against his

8. a promise or assurance: I give you my word I’ll return
